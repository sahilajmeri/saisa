First meet

It was on my birthday when saif had suprised me. He had planned suprise with my mom.He made my day more special by surprising me. And i still remember he gifted me the perfume. After meeting we both realised there are soo many things similar between us. That casual kiss on my cheek would have meant nothing up until recently, I realized I was in love with him. Not that, 'I love you, man,' type of love. Nope. I was teacup in love with my best friend. The 'let's get married and grow old together' type of love.

Proposal

June 4ᵗʰ 2015

So basically i was the one who took initiative in our relation i and saif were facebook friends and we both talked as a casual friends shared everything... After few months i thought there is something kind of liking between us and i thought we should take this relation further and saif is a very shy guy he liked me but he never confessed so i took the moment and proposed him on a call i still remember that moment when i was returning from a volleyball match and we were talking and i officially proposed him and in a minute he was like yes i do love you but i never confessed.That was on 4th June 2015. And suddenly all the love songs were for him... but there was always a question in our mind when we will meet?
